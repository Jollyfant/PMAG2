Paleomagnetism.org 2.0 - Homepage

Welcome to Paleomagnetism.org 2.0 - In this application we emphasize on the clear distinction between the interpretation and statistical analysis of derived components. Inside the interpretation portal, metadata is collected at the specimen level, and specimens can be combined to form a collection. This collection is a permanent data object that can be published in the Data Library and used as the input for the other statistics and geography portals.

Interpretation Portal

The interpretation portal accepts demagnetization data from many laboratory formats. This is the portal where data is interpreted and specimen collections are created.
